üè¢ Inter Hall Administration (IHA)

The Inter Hall Administration (IHA) is responsible for the management of hostel facilities, encompassing the entire hostel allocation process and the facilitation of FRRO registration.

üîß Hostel Warden System

Each hostel is administered by a team of four wardens responsible for addressing all matters related to accommodation, maintenance, sanitation, mess facilities, and other associated concerns.

1. Senior Warden

Chief executive for welfare, discipline, mess, security etc. Also usually handles Administration portfolio.

2. Warden (Mess)

Looks after hostel mess affairs: quality, bills, service etc.

3. Warden (Health & Recreation)

Responsible for health, hygienic living, and the recreational/common room activities.

4. Warden (Maintenance & Sanitation)

Oversees repairs, cleanliness, sanitation, maintenance of hostel infrastructure.

üë• Student Representation

  • Hostel President: Elected by residents of each hostel/wing, serving as the liaison between students and the administration.
  • Hostel Student Committee: Presidents and other student representatives to raise and address student queries, feedback, issues, and represent in IHA meetings.

üè• Health Centre Services

ü©∫ Key Facilities

OPD Consultations: General + Specialist consultations, including internal medicine, ENT, ophthalmology, skin, dentistry & more.
Laboratory Services: Routine blood, biochemistry, microbiology tests.
Pharmacy: Free medicines for students; emergency meds, injections, first-aid included.
Ambulance Services: Available 24-hours (ICU ambulance + patient transport vehicle).
Routine Services: Immunization (tetanus), medical examinations for academic purposes.

üÜî Health Card / ID Booklet
