About International Students Association

The International Students Association (ISA) is an official body of JNU, instituted in 1985 as the Foreign Students Association (FSA), renamed to ISA in 2011. It promotes cultural exchange among international and host students, serving 600+ students from 50 countries. ISA assists with integration, problem-solving, and community building, functioning democratically through elected committees.
